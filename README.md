# Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis & Dashboard (Excel)
## Project Objective
To analyze hotel booking data and identify the key factors influencing cancellations by performing data cleaning and exploratory analysis in Excel. The objective is to develop an interactive dashboard that highlights trends, patterns, and insights to support better decision-making and reduce cancellation rates.

##Project Insights
-The analysis of hotel booking data reveals clear patterns in customer behavior and cancellation trends. 
-City hotels experience significantly higher bookings and cancellations compared to resort hotels, indicating a more dynamic and less predictable customer base.
-Cancellations are notably higher among couples, suggesting that flexible travel plans may influence their decisions. 
-Monthly trends show fluctuations in both bookings and cancellations, with peak activity during mid-year months, highlighting the impact of seasonality. 
-Additionally, bookings with longer lead times and certain room preferences are more likely to be canceled, indicating that early planners may change their decisions more frequently.

##Conclusion
-Overall, the project demonstrates that booking cancellations are influenced by multiple factors such as hotel type, customer segment, and time of booking.
-By leveraging these insights through an interactive Excel dashboard, hotels can better understand customer behavior and take proactive measures—such as optimizing booking policies, targeting high-risk segments, and adjusting pricing strategies—to reduce cancellations and improve overall revenue performance.
